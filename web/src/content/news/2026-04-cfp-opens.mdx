The Call for Papers for **PGConf Poland 2026** is officially open.

We are looking for proposals across four formats — 25-minute talks,
45-minute talks, 90-minute workshops, and 5-minute lightning sessions —
on any topic relevant to people who build, run, or bet their products
on PostgreSQL. Beginner-friendly talks are explicitly welcome alongside
deep-internals work.

## The dates

- **CFP opens:** 15 April 2026
- **CFP closes:** 15 August 2026, 23:59 CEST
- **Notifications:** by 10 September 2026
- **Conference day:** 24 November 2026, ARCHE Dwór Uphagena Gdańsk

## What we're looking for

The programme committee reads every submission. Suggested topics this year:
PostgreSQL internals; performance and query tuning; replication and HA;
AI, embeddings, and pgvector; observability and monitoring; security and
compliance; cloud and managed Postgres; and migration stories — what
worked, what didn't, what you would do differently.

These are suggestions, not constraints. If your talk is honest, specific,
and useful, we want it.

## Speaker benefits

Free conference ticket, post-event speaker dinner in the Old Town, and a
travel support fund for speakers who could not otherwise attend — apply
in the submission form.

[Submit your proposal →](/cfp)

We will see you in Gdańsk.
