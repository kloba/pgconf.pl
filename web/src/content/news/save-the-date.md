We are thrilled to announce **PGConf Poland 2026** — the first PostgreSQL
Europe conference held in Poland — taking place on **24 November 2026** at
the historic **ARCHE Dwór Uphagena Gdańsk**, just a short walk from
Gdańsk's medieval Old Town.

The full programme — including the Call for Papers, ticket sales, and the
list of confirmed sponsors — will roll out across the spring and summer.

Subscribe to our updates and follow us on Mastodon to hear it first.
